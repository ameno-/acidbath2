## Master Copywriter Checklist

Analysis checks:

- [ ] **Authority established** - Credentials or research cited?
- [ ] **Social proof present** - Testimonials or adoption stats?
- [ ] **Story integrated** - Narrative illustrating benefits?
- [ ] **Claims backed** - Data or proof supporting assertions?
- [ ] **Credibility built** - Trust signals throughout?
- [ ] **Psychology applied** - Cialdini principles used?
- [ ] **Value-first** - Give before asking?
- [ ] **Technical preserved** - Code/data unchanged?
